Warns System
============

Keep track of `User` warns by using `Mecha Karens` Simple but Powerful warn management system.

.. Important::
	* Each warn can only have a maximum of 100 characters
	* A User can recieve up 20 warns
	
	* You can't warn yourself
	* You can't warn bots
	* You can't warn members with a higher role
